Kronk’s Spinach Puffs from The Emperor’s New Groove

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of fresh spinach, roughly chopped
  • 1 cup of ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • A pinch of nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper, just a dash
  • 1 package of puff pastry sheets, thawed
  • A splash of olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a skillet over medium heat, add a splash of olive oil and sauté the spinach until just wilted, about 2 minutes. Let it cool slightly.
  3. In a mixing bowl, combine the sautéed spinach, ricotta, Parmesan, beaten egg, nutmeg, salt, and pepper. Mix until well incorporated.
  4. Unfold the puff pastry sheets on a lightly floured surface and cut into 12 equal squares.
  5. Spoon a tablespoon of the spinach mixture onto the center of each pastry square.
  6. Fold the pastry over the filling to form a triangle, pressing the edges firmly with a fork to seal.
  7. Place the puffs on the prepared baking sheet and brush the tops with a bit of the remaining beaten egg for a golden finish.
  8. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the puffs are puffed up and golden brown.

Grey Stuff from Beauty and the Beast

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of vanilla pudding mix
  • 2 cups of milk
  • A splash of vanilla extract
  • A couple of drops of black food coloring
  • 1/2 cup of crushed chocolate cookies
  • A handful of edible silver pearls for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together the vanilla pudding mix and milk until smooth, ensuring no lumps remain for a silky texture.
  2. Stir in a splash of vanilla extract for an aromatic depth, then add a couple of drops of black food coloring, mixing until you achieve a uniform grey hue.
  3. Fold in the crushed chocolate cookies gently, allowing some texture to remain for a delightful crunch.
  4. Chill the mixture in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, or until it’s set firmly, to let the flavors meld beautifully.
  5. Before serving, sprinkle a handful of edible silver pearls on top for a shimmering finish that’s as magical as the dish’s origins.

Lilo’s Peanut Butter Sandwiches from Lilo & Stitch

Ingredients

  • A couple of slices of soft white bread
  • A generous smear of creamy peanut butter
  • A drizzle of honey
  • A sprinkle of crushed macadamia nuts
  • A splash of vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Lay out two slices of soft white bread on a clean, flat surface.
  2. Using a butter knife, spread a generous amount of creamy peanut butter evenly on one side of each slice.
  3. Lightly drizzle honey over the peanut butter on one slice for a touch of sweetness.
  4. Sprinkle a small amount of crushed macadamia nuts over the honey for a crunchy texture and a nod to Hawaiian flavors.
  5. Add a splash of vanilla extract to the other slice of peanut butter-covered bread, spreading it gently to incorporate.
  6. Press the two slices together, peanut butter sides facing in, to form your sandwich.
  7. For an extra touch, trim the crusts off the sandwich and cut it into fun shapes to appeal to the kid in everyone.
